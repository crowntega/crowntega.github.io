Stephanie's Founders Story

I am Stephanie. I am from a family of four, I am a badminton player and I am a rising android developer; I learned to develop simple apps thanks to the Andela Learning Community. I am a business and marketing major. Before I came to Ghana I founded a very young company called Vibrant High Peak. Within Vibrant High Peak, is a sub division that sells unique greeting cards in the four major languages of Nigeria:

  • Yoruba
  • Hausa
  • Igbo
  • Pidgin

My goal for the company was to be the African Hallmark. I planned to expand the business into other sub-Saharan countries before I was admitted into Mest; for now the company is under suspension.
